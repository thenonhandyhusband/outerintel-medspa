Section 5

Your 90-Day Action Plan

Prioritized by effort vs. impact — start at the top
Quick Wins — Week 1–2
Add a Botox pricing FAQ to your Google Business Profile and website — even a range eliminates the #1 complaint driving 1-star reviews at competitors
Update your Google Business Profile description with "natural results" and "we take time to answer your questions" — exact language from 5-star competitor reviews
Set a 24-hour review response policy — reply to all new Google reviews within one business day; competitors average 3–5 days
Medium-Term — Month 1–2
Launch a post-visit review request via SMS or email — target 3+ new reviews per week to close the velocity gap with Luxe Skin Lounge within 60 days
Start a before/after photo workflow for every patient who consents — Luxe Skin Lounge's review volume spike correlates directly with before/after content mentions
Add a "Free Consultation" page to your website emphasizing the photo review process — reviews explicitly cite consults as a booking trigger for top-rated competitors
Strategic — Month 2–3
If you offer RF skin tightening (Morpheus8, Sofwave, etc.), build a dedicated landing page — this is unclaimed territory in your market and has zero competitor content competing for search
Test a monthly peel membership model — peel patients have the highest return rate in your competitor review set; a membership captures that LTV
Brief your front desk team on the top complaint pattern — patients who feel "rushed" or "upselled at checkout" almost never return; build a post-treatment script that sets expectations without pressure
